How It Works

Four steps. No account needed. Your phone is your wallet.

📱
Step 1

Scan

Scan a barcode or photograph a price tag. The app reads the EAN and price automatically.

📍
Step 2

Locate

GPS captures your exact location. Price + coordinates are hashed client-side with SHA-256.

⛓️
Step 3

Anchor

The hash is anchored on Base L2 blockchain. Immutable. Transparent. ~$0.001 per transaction.

🎁
Step 4

Earn

Earn IFR token credits for every verified submission. First movers earn 2x bonus rewards.

No new token. Vendetta uses IFR as the reward currency.

How Rewards Work

Every verified price submission earns IFR credits. First movers earn 2x. IFR lockers earn +20%.

Credits accumulate off-chain. Claim on-chain when you reach 1,000 credits via VendClaim.sol on Base L2.
